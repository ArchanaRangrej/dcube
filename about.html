About

We are one stop solution for Custom Interior Architecture Graphics & Home Furnishing

A house becomes a home when a family occupies it. And the proof of human life in a home is discernible in its decor and furnishings. The adornment of a living space reflects the personalities and lifestyles of its occupants. Every piece of furniture hints at the fact that 'this is a home and people live in it.' In other words, your home decor and furnishings are a symbol of your very existence and your warmth is imprinted upon them.

Hence, it makes perfect sense to ensure that the interior design and decorations of your home are aesthetically sound. It is only natural that you would want your home to be beautiful, impressive, and a place worthy of your residence. But the question remains, where do you go to achieve this goal?

You come to us, of course. At D~CUBE, we strive to provide the best of custom interior architecture graphics and custom home furnishing services, so that you can add that personal touch to your home space. After all, every aspect of your home should mirror the life you live with your family.

You might want to know how we came to be. It started with a friendship between three friends who pooled in their minds and resources to give birth to D~CUBE. With our three Ds that stand for Design, Detail and Decor, we have found a place in the furniture and interior design world. We're a team of result-oriented professionals who have garnered more than ten years of experience in our field. We live to satisfy our customers, so you can be sure that you and your home are in the right hands. We are ever ready to tackle new and challenging projects; to give our very best for our customers' sake.
